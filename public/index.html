Introduction

Summoner's War is an RPG mobile game available on android and IOS. It's considered a "gacha" game, meaning it has loot boxes in which the desired items are quite rare, kind of like a toy vending machine. For example, the rate to receive a "nat 5" monster (the strongest characters in the game) are 1 in 200 mystical scrolls. Furthermore, there are light and dark characters, which can be summoned from light and dark scrolls, and the rates for these are even worse, at 1 in 300 light and dark scrolls. Besides collecting monsters, there are also runes and artifacts, which can be equipped on a monster to give them stats and abilities. As you can imagine, the good runes are very hard to come by, making Summoner's War one of the grindiest games in its genre.
